parent_command:crypto/dd
usage: balance [--vs {USDT,TUSD,USDC,BUSD,RUB,TRY,EUR,ZAR,GBP,UAH,BIDR,DAI,BRL,PLN,RON}]

Display balance

optional arguments:
  --vs {USDT,TUSD,USDC,BUSD,RUB,TRY,EUR,ZAR,GBP,UAH,BIDR,DAI,BRL,PLN,RON}
                        Quote currency (what to view coin vs) (default: USDT)


Examples:
- To view the balance of a specific coin against USDT: crypto/dd/balance
- Display the balance of a coin in comparison to EUR: crypto/dd/balance --vs EUR
- Check the balance of a cryptocurrency against GBP: crypto/dd/balance --vs GBP
- See the balance of a digital asset compared to BRL: crypto/dd/balance --vs BRL
- Examine the balance of a coin in terms of DAI: crypto/dd/balance --vs DAI
- Assess the balance of a cryptocurrency against RUB: crypto/dd/balance --vs RUB
- Review the balance of a digital currency in comparison to PLN: crypto/dd/balance --vs PLN
- Inspect the balance of a coin against ZAR: crypto/dd/balance --vs ZAR
- Analyze the balance of a cryptocurrency in relation to RON: crypto/dd/balance --vs RON
- Observe the balance of a digital asset compared to UAH: crypto/dd/balance --vs UAH
