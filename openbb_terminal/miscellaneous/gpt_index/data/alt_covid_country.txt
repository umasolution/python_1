parent_command:alt/covid
usage: country [-c country_name]

Select a country to look at data for.

optional arguments:
  -c country_name, --country country_name
                        Country to get data for. (default: None)


Examples:
- To view COVID data for a specific country, use: alt/covid/country -c <country_name>
- For COVID data in the United States, enter: alt/covid/country --country United States
- To check COVID data in India, type: alt/covid/country -c India
- Get COVID data for Brazil with this command: alt/covid/country --country Brazil
- To access COVID data for the United Kingdom, input: alt/covid/country -c United Kingdom
