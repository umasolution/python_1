parent_command:portfolio
usage: maxdd

Show portfolio maximum drawdown

maxdd
Examples:
- To display the maximum drawdown of your portfolio: portfolio/maxdd
- Show the maximum drawdown for your current portfolio: portfolio/maxdd
- Retrieve the maximum drawdown value for your portfolio: portfolio/maxdd
- Get the highest drawdown experienced by your portfolio: portfolio/maxdd
- Check the maximum drawdown in your portfolio: portfolio/maxdd
