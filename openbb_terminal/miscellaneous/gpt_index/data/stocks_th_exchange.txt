Summary: Display the open hours for a specific stock exchange. You can select the exchange by providing its short name.

Examples:
- To see the open hours for the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE): stocks/th/exchange -n NYSE
- Check the open hours for the London Stock Exchange (LSE): stocks/th/exchange --name LSE
- Find the open hours for the Tokyo Stock Exchange (TWO): stocks/th/exchange -n TWO
- Get the open hours for the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX): stocks/th/exchange --name ASX
