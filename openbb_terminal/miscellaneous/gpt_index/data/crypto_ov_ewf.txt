usage: ewf

Display exchange withdrawal fees [Source: https://withdrawalfees.com/]

ewf
Examples:
- To view exchange withdrawal fees for a specific cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/ov/ewf
- Display exchange withdrawal fees for a selected coin: crypto/ov/ewf <COIN>
- Check withdrawal fees for a particular cryptocurrency: crypto/ov/ewf <COIN>
- Retrieve exchange withdrawal fees for a specific digital currency: crypto/load <COIN>/ov/ewf
