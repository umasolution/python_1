parent_command:stocks/options/hedge
usage: sop

Displays selected option

sop
Examples:
- Display selected option for a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/hedge/sop
- To view the chosen option for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/hedge/sop
- Show the selected option after loading a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/hedge/sop
- For a specific stock, display the chosen option: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/hedge/sop
- After loading a stock, show its selected option: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/options/hedge/sop
