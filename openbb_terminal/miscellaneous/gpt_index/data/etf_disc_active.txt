parent_command:etf/disc
usage: active

Displays most active ETF/Mutual funds from wsj.com/market-data

active
Examples:
- To display the most active ETFs and mutual funds: etf/disc/active
- Show the most active ETFs and mutual funds: etf/disc/active
- View the top active ETFs and mutual funds: etf/disc/active
- Retrieve the most active ETFs and mutual funds from WSJ Market Data: etf/disc/active
- Get a list of the most active ETFs and mutual funds: etf/disc/active
