parent_command:econometrics
usage: remove [-n {}]

Remove a dataset from the loaded dataset list

optional arguments:
  -n {}, --name {}  The name of the dataset you want to remove (default: None)


Examples:
- Remove a dataset from the loaded list by its name: econometrics/remove -n {}
- To delete a specific dataset, use: econometrics/remove --name {}
- Erase a dataset from the list with: econometrics/remove -n {}
- To get rid of a particular dataset, execute: econometrics/remove --name {}
