parent_command:stocks/qa
usage: summary

Summary statistics

summary
Examples:
- Retrieve summary statistics for a loaded stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/summary
- Obtain summary statistics of a stock after loading it: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/summary
- Load a stock symbol and get its summary statistics: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/summary
- Access summary statistics for a loaded stock symbol: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/summary
- Load a stock and display its summary statistics: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/summary
- What was the mean return for SYMBOL over the last year: stocks/load <SYMBOL> -s <ONE YEAR AGO>/qa/summary
