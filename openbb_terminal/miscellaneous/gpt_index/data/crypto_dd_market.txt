parent_command:crypto/dd
usage: market

Market data for loaded coin. There you find metrics like: Market Cap, Supply, Circulating Supply, Price, Volume and many others.

market
Examples:
- To view market data for a specific coin: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/market
- Obtain market metrics for the loaded cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/market
- Access market information for a selected coin: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/market
- Retrieve market data of the desired cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/market
- Display market data for a particular coin: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/market
