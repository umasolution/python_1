parent_command:alt/covid
usage: rates

Show historical death/cases rates for a country.

rates
Examples:
- To display historical death and cases rates for a specific country, use: alt/covid/rates <COUNTRY>
- Obtain the historical death and cases rates for the United States: alt/covid/rates United States
- Show historical death and cases rates for France: alt/covid/rates France
- Retrieve the historical death and cases rates for India: alt/covid/rates India
- Get the historical death and cases rates for Brazil: alt/covid/rates Brazil
