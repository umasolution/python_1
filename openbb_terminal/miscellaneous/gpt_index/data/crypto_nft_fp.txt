parent_command:crypto/nft
usage: fp -s SLUG

Display floor price of a certain NFT collection. [Source: https://nftpricefloor.com/]

optional arguments:
  -s SLUG, --slug SLUG  NFT floor price collection slug (e.g., bored-ape-yacht-club) (default: None)


Examples:
- To view the floor price of a specific NFT collection, use: crypto/nft/fp -s <SLUG>
- Display the floor price for the Bored Ape Yacht Club collection: crypto/nft/fp -s bored-ape-yacht-club
- Retrieve the floor price of the World of Women NFT collection: crypto/nft/fp -s world-of-women
- Check the floor price for the Pudgy Penguins collection: crypto/nft/fp -s pudgy-penguins
- Get the floor price for the Rumble Kong League collection: crypto/nft/fp -s rumble-kong-league
