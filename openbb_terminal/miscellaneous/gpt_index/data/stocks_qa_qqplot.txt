Display QQ plot vs normal quantiles

qqplot
Examples:
- To display a QQ plot for a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/qqplot
- Show a QQ plot for the selected stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/qqplot
- Visualize the QQ plot of a stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/qqplot
- Generate a QQ plot for the loaded stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/qqplot
