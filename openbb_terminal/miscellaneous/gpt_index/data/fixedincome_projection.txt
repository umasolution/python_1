parent_command:fixedincome
usage: projection [-l]

Get the Federal Reserve's projection of the federal funds rate.

optional arguments:
  -l, --long-run  Whether to show the long run projection (default: False)


Examples:
- To view the Federal Reserve's projection of the federal funds rate: fixedincome/projection
- To display the long run projection of the federal funds rate: fixedincome/projection -l
