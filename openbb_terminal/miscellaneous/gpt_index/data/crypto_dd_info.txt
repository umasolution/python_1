parent_command:crypto/dd
usage: info

Shows basic information about loaded coin like: Name, Symbol, Description, Market Cap, Public Interest, Supply, and Price related metrics

info
Examples:
- To display basic information about a loaded coin: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/info
- Show information about a specific cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/info
- Retrieve basic details of a loaded coin: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/info
- Access essential details for a loaded cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/info
- Obtain basic metrics for a specific coin: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/info
