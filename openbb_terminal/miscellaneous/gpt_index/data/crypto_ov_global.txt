parent_command:crypto/ov
usage: global [--pie]

Shows global statistics about Crypto Market

optional arguments:
  --pie  Flag to show pie chart with market cap distribution. Works only with CoinGecko source (default: False)


Examples:
- To display global statistics about the Crypto Market: crypto/ov global
- To show a pie chart with market cap distribution for the Crypto Market: crypto/ov global --pie
