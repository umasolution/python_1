parent_command:stocks/qa
usage: bw [-y]

Box and Whisker plot

optional arguments:
  -y, --yearly  Flag to show yearly bw plot (default: False)


Examples:
- To display a box and whisker plot for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/bw
- To show a yearly box and whisker plot for <SYMBOL>: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/bw -y
