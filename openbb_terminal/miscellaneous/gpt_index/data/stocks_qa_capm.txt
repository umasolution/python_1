parent_command:stocks/qa
usage: capm

Provides detailed information about a stock's risk compared to the market risk.

capm
Examples:
- To obtain detailed information about a stock's risk compared to the market risk: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/capm
- For CAPM analysis of a specific stock: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/capm
- Analyze a stock's risk relative to market risk: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/capm
- Assess the risk of a stock compared to the market: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/capm
- Determine the risk of a stock in relation to the market risk: stocks/load <SYMBOL>/qa/capm
