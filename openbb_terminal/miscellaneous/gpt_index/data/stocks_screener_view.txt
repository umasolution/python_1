parent_command:stocks/screener
usage: view [-p Desired preset.]

View available presets under presets folder.

optional arguments:
  -p Desired preset., --preset Desired preset.
                        View specific custom preset


Examples:
- To view all available presets: stocks/screener/view
- To view a specific custom preset: stocks/screener/view -p <Desired preset>
- Display a particular preset: stocks/screener/view --preset <Desired preset>
