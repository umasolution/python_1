Display asset's investors [Source: https://messari.io]

inv
Examples:
- To display the investors of a specific cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/inv
- Show the list of investors for a particular crypto asset: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/inv
- Retrieve the investors associated with a specific cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/inv
- Obtain the list of investors for a given crypto asset: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/inv
- Access the investors information for a particular cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/inv
