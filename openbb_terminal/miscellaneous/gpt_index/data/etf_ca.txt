Compare ETF's holdings with each other

ca
Examples:
- Compare holdings of two ETFs: etf/load <SYMBOL1>/ca <SYMBOL2>
- Analyze the differences in holdings between two ETFs: etf/load <SYMBOL1>/ca <SYMBOL2>
- Examine the holdings comparison of two ETFs: etf/load <SYMBOL1>/ca <SYMBOL2>
- Assess the similarities and differences in holdings of two ETFs: etf/load <SYMBOL1>/ca <SYMBOL2>
- Investigate the holdings comparison for a pair of ETFs: etf/load <SYMBOL1>/ca <SYMBOL2>
