parent_command:crypto/dd
usage: fundrate

Displays funding rate by exchange for a certain asset [Source: https://coinglass.github.io/API-Reference/]

fundrate
Examples:
- To view the funding rate for a specific cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/fundrate
- Display funding rate for a chosen asset: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/fundrate
- Show funding rate by exchange for a particular cryptocurrency: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/fundrate
- Retrieve the funding rate for a selected digital asset: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/fundrate
- Check the funding rate for a certain crypto asset by exchange: crypto/load <COIN>/dd/fundrate
