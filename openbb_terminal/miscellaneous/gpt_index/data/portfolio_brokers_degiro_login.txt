parent_command:portfolio/brokers/degiro
usage: login [-otp ONE_TIME_PASSWORD]

optional arguments:
  -otp ONE_TIME_PASSWORD, --one-time-password ONE_TIME_PASSWORD
                        One-time-password for 2FA.


Examples:
- To log in to your Degiro account without 2FA: portfolio/brokers/degiro/login
- To log in to your Degiro account with a one-time password for 2FA: portfolio/brokers/degiro/login -otp <ONE_TIME_PASSWORD>
