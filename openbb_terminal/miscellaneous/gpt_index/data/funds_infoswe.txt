parent_command:funds
usage: infoswe

Show fund info of a swedish fund. To get a list of available funds, check the file `avanza_fund_ID.csv`.

infoswe
Examples:
- Display information for a Swedish fund by its ID: funds/infoswe <FUND_ID>
- To find the available Swedish funds and their IDs, refer to the `avanza_fund_ID.csv` file, and then use the fund ID to get its information: funds/infoswe <FUND_ID>
