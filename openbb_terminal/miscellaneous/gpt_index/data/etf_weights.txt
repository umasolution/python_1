parent_command:etf
usage: weights

Look at ETF sector holdings

weights
Examples:
- To view the sector holdings of an ETF: etf/weights
- Examine the sector weights of a specific ETF by loading it first: etf/load <SYMBOL>/weights
- Analyze the sector holdings in an ETF after loading the desired ETF: etf/load <SYMBOL>/weights
- Load an ETF symbol and then inspect its sector weights: etf/load <SYMBOL>/weights
- After loading the ETF symbol, view its sector holdings: etf/load <SYMBOL>/weights
